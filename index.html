About Me

I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from Dayananda Sagar University, Bengaluru (CGPA: 7.65, First Class, 2023). My academics covered algorithms, data structures, NLP, software engineering, and data science.

After graduation, I joined LTIMindtree as a Package Implementation Consultant in SAP Basis — working on S/4HANA migrations, system performance optimisation, and enterprise IT infrastructure. I received the Star Performer Award and multiple Super Crew Awards.

My interest in HCI grew from observing how complex enterprise systems frustrate users — not due to technology, but poor interface design. I want to bridge this gap by studying Human Computer Interaction.

My thesis — "Detection of Thyroid Disorder using Machine Learning" — strengthened my belief in building systems that are not only technically sound but also accessible and usable by non-technical users.
